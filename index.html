🎯 What I Do

MCU firmware architecture and development for vehicle controllers. CAN-bus communication design, FreeRTOS/RT-Thread multi-tasking, and production-level debugging — from prototype to mass production.

🛠 Core Stack

📂 Engineering Style

🌱 Learning

RTOS kernel internals, embedded GUI frameworks, and CI/CD for firmware. I distill field experience into reusable reference repos.
